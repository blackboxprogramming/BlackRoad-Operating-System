## Key Journey Insights

### 1. Identity as Central Hub
Every journey converges on **blackroad.me** - it's the persistent anchor across all touchpoints.

### 2. Multiple Valid Entry Points
- Enterprise: blackroad.systems
- Developer: blackroad.network
- Creator: lucidia.earth
- Researcher: blackroadquantum.com

All roads lead to the same coherent universe.

### 3. Progressive Depth
- **Surface:** Beautiful, inviting (Lucidia, system homepage)
- **Mid-depth:** Product and docs (Network, AI console)
- **Deep:** Architecture and research (Systems architecture, Quantum)

Users can go as deep as they want.

### 4. Cross-Journey Opportunities
- Enterprise customer's developers become Network power users
- Developer's company becomes Enterprise customer
- Student becomes employee becomes advocate
- Researcher's work improves product credibility

### 5. Lucidia as Universal Guide
Lucidia can appear across all journeys as:
- Helper in blackroad.me
- Onboarding guide in blackroadai.com
- Storyteller in lucidia.earth
- Friendly face for newcomers everywhere
