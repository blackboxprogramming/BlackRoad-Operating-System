## Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks & Mitigations

**Risk 1: Enterprise sales cycle too long**
**Mitigation:**
- Start design partner outreach in Month 1
- Hybrid model: fast developer adoption + slow enterprise
- Focus on smaller enterprises first ($50K deals vs $500K)

**Risk 2: Developer adoption too slow**
**Mitigation:**
- Obsess over first 5-minute experience
- Active community engagement
- Open-source strategic components
- Content marketing and education

**Risk 3: ALICE QI not differentiated enough**
**Mitigation:**
- Heavy research investment
- Academic partnerships for credibility
- Clear technical whitepapers
- Benchmark against alternatives

**Risk 4: Too many products/domains to execute**
**Mitigation:**
- Strict phase discipline (don't jump ahead)
- Focus on Phase 1 success before expanding
- Hire strategically for Phase 2/3 needs

**Risk 5: Competitive pressure**
**Mitigation:**
- Move fast in Phase 1 (land grab)
- Deep moats: determinism, audit trails, research
- Enterprise lock-in via compliance
- Community and ecosystem effects
